I have always loved quotes. And, I honestly think everyone does as they have a means of so concisely and eloquently conveying exactly what we're thinking. Although this blog is not exactly a typical blog, here are some of my favorite quotes, and I hope you'll share some of yours with me!


"You never really understand a person until you consider things from his point of view. Until you climb into his skin and walk around in it." -Atticus Finch, To Kill a Mockingbird

"Be somebody who makes everybody feel like a somebody."

"Surround yourself with people who see your value and remind you of it."

"Confidence is not 'they will like me.' Confidence is 'I'll be fine if they don't.'"

"Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss you'll land among the stars."

"You can't go back and change the beginning, but you can start where you are and change the ending."

"You were born to be real, not to be perfect."

"What holds you back is not what you are, it's what you think you aren't."

"The way I see it, if you want the rainbow, you gotta put up with the rain."
